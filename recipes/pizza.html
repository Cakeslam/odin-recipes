Pizza Recipe

Description

Like pizza? Then you'll love this recipe.

Combining rich tomato sauce with gooey mozzarella and succulent pepperoni, you won't want to order pizza ever again!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepeproni
  1. Roll the dough into a circle that has a diameter of around 9 inches, and a thickness of around 1cm.
  2. Spread the tomato sauce evenly, leaving about an inch of bare dough around the edge.
  3. Sprinkle the cheese evenly over the pizza.
  4. Arrange the pepperoni to taste.
  5. Cook the pizza in the oven at 200 degrees celcius for 8-10 minutes.
