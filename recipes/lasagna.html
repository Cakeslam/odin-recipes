Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is lasagna just like your mama used to make!

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Beef mince
  • Onions
  • Tomato sauce
  • White sauce
  • Cheddar cheese
  1. Brown the mince.
  2. Add the onions and cook until soft.
  3. Add the tomato sauce to coat the mince.
  4. Add a layer of the mince to an oven dish.
  5. Place pasta sheets over the mince to create another layer.
  6. Add a layer of white sauce.
  7. Repeat until you have almost filled the dish.
  8. Sprinkle cheese on top as desired.
  9. Cook in the oven at 200 degrees Celcius for 25-30 minutes.
